# Best of 2025: Movies, TV shows, & music

**Source:** https://www.oppl.org/news-events/authors-books/best-of-movies-tv-music/
**Date:** December 3, 2025

## Summary

The Oak Park Library has released its annual staff-curated roundup of entertainment recommendations for 2025, featuring librarians' favorite books, movies, TV shows, and music across all age groups. The comprehensive list showcases staff picks ranging from animated series like "Arcane, Season 2" to critically acclaimed films such as "Sinners," which multiple staff members called a "masterpiece," and music albums from artists like Doechii and Franz Ferdinand.

The recommendations span diverse genres and interests, with librarians providing personal insights about why each selection stands out. Notable highlights include praise for "Flow," a wordless animated animal movie that moved staff to tears, "The Gilded Age" season 3 for its stunning costumes and historical drama, and "Thunderbolts" for its honest depiction of mental health in superhero storytelling. The music selections range from hip-hop and K-pop soundtracks to folk and electronic genres, demonstrating the breadth of staff expertise.

This annual roundup serves Oak Park residents by offering curated entertainment options backed by professional librarian recommendations, helping community members discover new content across books, films, television, and music that they might not have found otherwise. The list is organized by category and age group, making it easy for residents to find recommendations suited to their specific interests and reading levels.
