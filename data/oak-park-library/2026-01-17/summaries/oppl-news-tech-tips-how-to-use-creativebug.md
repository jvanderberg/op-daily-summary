# Tech Tips: How to use Creativebug

**Source:** https://www.oppl.org/news-events/digital-learning/tech-tips-how-to-use-creativebug/
**Date:** November 14, 2025

## Summary

This article introduces Oak Park library patrons to Creativebug, a digital crafting and DIY resource now available through the library system. Creativebug offers more than 1,000 award-winning art and craft video classes taught by recognized design experts, covering topics like art, sewing, cooking techniques, knitting, paper crafts, and more.

The article provides step-by-step instructions for Oak Park residents to access the service using their library card number and PIN through the library's website portal. Users can create accounts, browse classes by category, save videos to watchlists, and follow structured "learning journeys" that build skills progressively. Popular classes highlighted include basic line drawing, watercolor, creative boot camp exercises, and various craft projects like zine-making and sewing techniques.

This new digital resource expands Oak Park library services beyond physical materials, offering residents free access to professional-quality creative instruction they can pursue at their own pace from home. The service caters to all skill levels, from beginners to experienced crafters looking to learn new techniques.
