# Best of 2025: Teen fiction & nonfiction

**Source:** https://www.oppl.org/news-events/authors-books/best-of-teens/
**Date:** December 3, 2025

## Summary

The Oak Park Public Library has released their annual staff-curated roundup of recommended books, TV shows, movies, and music for 2025. This comprehensive guide features librarian picks across all age groups and interests, with dedicated sections for adult fiction (general, romance, and genre categories), adult nonfiction, teen fiction and nonfiction, children's books, picture books, movies and TV shows, and music.

The teen fiction and nonfiction section showcases a diverse range of titles, from graphic novels like "Absolute Wonder Woman" and "Angelica & the Bear Prince" to thrillers such as "The Meadowbrook Murders" and non-fiction works like "Death in the Jungle" about Jonestown. Notable highlights include Suzanne Collins' return to The Hunger Games series with "Sunrise on the Reaping," focusing on Haymitch Abernathy, and various other titles spanning fantasy, romance, and contemporary issues.

This annual guide serves as a valuable resource for Oak Park residents looking for their next great read or entertainment recommendation, with each title featuring personal endorsements from library staff members who explain why readers should give these works a try.
