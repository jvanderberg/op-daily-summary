# Best of 2025: Kids & picture books

**Source:** https://www.oppl.org/news-events/authors-books/best-of-kids/
**Date:** December 3, 2025

## Summary

Oak Park Public Library has released its annual staff-curated book and media recommendations for 2025, featuring favorites across all ages and genres. The comprehensive list includes staff picks for kids' books, picture books, adult fiction and nonfiction, teen materials, movies, TV shows, and music, with librarians and staff members providing personal endorsements for each selection.

The kids' book recommendations showcase diverse stories including "Bad Bunny" by Claudia Romo Edelman from the Hispanic Star series, Kate DiCamillo's fairytale "Lost Evangeline," and "Mixed-Up" by Kami Garcia, which addresses dyslexia and learning differences. Other notable picks include "Vanya & the Wild Hunt," a fantasy adventure featuring a British Indian protagonist with ADHD, and "A World Without Summer," a nonfiction book about the volcanic eruption that inspired Mary Shelley to write Frankenstein.

The picture book selections range from imaginative adventures like "Cat Nap" by Brian Lies to educational titles such as "A Gift of Dust" about how Saharan sand nourishes the Amazon rainforest. This annual roundup serves as a valuable resource for Oak Park families looking for quality reading material, with each recommendation including personal insights from library staff about why readers should explore these titles.
