# Best of 2025: Adult fiction & nonfiction

**Source:** https://www.oppl.org/news-events/authors-books/best-of-adults/
**Date:** December 3, 2025

## Summary

The Oak Park Public Library has released its annual staff-curated roundup of favorite books, TV shows, movies, and music for 2025. Library staff members across different departments have compiled recommendations spanning all age groups and interests, from adult fiction and nonfiction to children's books, teen selections, and multimedia entertainment.

The adult fiction recommendations showcase diverse genres, including literary fiction like Taylor Jenkins Reid's "Atmosphere" (about female astronauts in the 1980s), horror selections such as Chuck Tingle's "Lucky Day," and romance titles like Jasmine Guillory's "Flirting Lessons." Staff members provide personal insights about why they recommend each book, with many noting compelling storytelling, unique perspectives, and emotional resonance. The nonfiction selections cover topics ranging from memoirs like Shari Franke's "The House of My Mother" to cultural explorations such as Robin Wall Kimmerer's "The Serviceberry."

This comprehensive reading and viewing guide serves Oak Park residents by offering curated recommendations from knowledgeable library professionals who understand diverse community interests. The roundup includes links to browse additional categories for teens, kids, picture books, and multimedia content, making it a valuable resource for residents seeking their next great read or entertainment option. The personal commentary from librarians adds authenticity and helps readers find titles that match their specific preferences and reading goals.
