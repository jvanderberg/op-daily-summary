# Ways to watch Oscar-nominated films

**Source:** https://www.oppl.org/news-events/arts-culture/how-to-watch-oscar-nominated-films/
**Date:** January 29, 2026

## Summary

The Oak Park Public Library is hosting its 16th Annual Oscarthon movie marathon in celebration of the 98th Academy Awards, which will air on Sunday, March 15. The two-day event will take place at the Main Library on Saturday, March 7 and Saturday, March 14, featuring Oscar-nominated films from the past year along with complimentary popcorn and raffle prizes for attendees.

In addition to the in-person marathon, Oak Park residents can access Academy Award nominees and winners through the library's digital streaming services, including Kanopy and Hoopla, using their library cards. The library is encouraging residents to register for the Oscarthon event and browse their collection of 2025 and 2026 films, though the complete film lineup for both days has not yet been announced and will be posted at a later date.
