# Park District of Oak Park Earns National Accreditation in Parks and Recreation

**Source:** https://pdop.org/news/park-district-of-oak-park-earns-national-accreditation-in-parks-and-recreation/
**Date:** October 21, 2025

## Summary

The Park District of Oak Park has achieved a prestigious milestone by earning national accreditation through the Commission for Accreditation of Park and Recreation Agencies (CAPRA) and the National Recreation and Park Association (NRPA). This distinguished accomplishment was announced during the 2025 NRPA Annual Conference in Orlando, Florida on October 21, 2025.

CAPRA accreditation is the only national accreditation available for park and recreation agencies and serves as a measure of an agency's overall quality of operation, management, and service to the community. To earn this recognition, the Park District of Oak Park had to demonstrate compliance with 154 rigorous standards and document all policies and procedures related to management and administration of lands, facilities, resources, programs, safety, and services.

This accreditation places Oak Park among an elite group of park and recreation agencies nationwide and internationally, indicating the district's commitment to providing the highest level of service to the community. For Oak Park residents, this recognition ensures that their park district meets the most stringent national standards for quality operations and service delivery, which translates into better programs, facilities, and overall recreation experiences for the community's 54,000 residents.
