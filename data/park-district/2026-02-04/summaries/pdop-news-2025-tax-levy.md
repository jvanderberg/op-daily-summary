# 2025 Tax Levy

**Source:** https://pdop.org/news/2025-tax-levy/
**Date:** October 17, 2025

## Summary

The Park District of Oak Park has announced a public hearing regarding the adoption of its 2025 Property Tax Levy. The hearing is scheduled for November 6, 2025, at 7:30 p.m. at the Hedges Administration Center, located at 218 Madison Street in Oak Park. The hearing will take place during the Board of Commissioners' committee of the whole meeting.

The notice was issued by Commissioner Sandy Lentz, Secretary of the Board of Commissioners for the Park District of Oak Park. This public hearing provides an opportunity for Oak Park residents to learn about and provide input on the park district's proposed property tax levy for the upcoming year, which will help fund various recreational facilities and programs throughout the community.
