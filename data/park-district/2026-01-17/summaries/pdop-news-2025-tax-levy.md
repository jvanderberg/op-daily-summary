# 2025 Tax Levy

**Source:** https://pdop.org/news/2025-tax-levy/
**Date:** October 17, 2025

## Summary

**Oak Park Park District Announces Public Hearing for 2025 Tax Levy**

The Park District of Oak Park has announced a public hearing regarding the adoption of its 2025 Property Tax Levy. The hearing will be conducted by the Board of Commissioners on November 6, 2025, at 7:30 p.m. at the Hedges Administration Center, located at 218 Madison Street in Oak Park.

The public hearing will take place during the Board of Commissioners' committee of the whole meeting, providing residents an opportunity to learn about and provide input on the park district's tax levy proposal for the upcoming year. The notice was issued by Commissioner Sandy Lentz, who serves as Secretary of the Board of Commissioners for the Park District of Oak Park.
