# Weekly Wrap-Up: Oct. 17, 2025

**Date:** October 17, 2025

## Summary

**Oak Park Elementary School District 97 Announces 2025-26 Academic Calendar**

Oak Park Elementary School District 97 has released its academic calendar for the 2025-26 school year. Students will begin classes on Wednesday, August 20, 2025, following two teacher institute days on August 18-19. The school year will conclude on Tuesday, June 2, 2026, providing 176 total student attendance days organized into three trimesters of 58, 58, and 60 days respectively.

The calendar includes several breaks throughout the year: Thanksgiving break from November 26-28, 2025; winter break from December 22, 2025 through January 2, 2026; and spring break from March 30 through April 3, 2026. Family-teacher conferences are scheduled for October 23-24, 2025, and February 12-13, 2026, with Thursday featuring full-day student attendance and evening conferences, while Friday will be staff-only with morning conferences. The district has also built in five emergency days (June 3-9, 2026) if needed due to weather or other closures.

This calendar provides Oak Park families with important planning dates for the upcoming school year, including trimester end dates, report card distribution schedules, and all major holidays when schools will be closed. The structured timeline ensures adequate instructional time while incorporating necessary breaks and professional development opportunities for staff.
