**Date:** November 21, 2025

## Summary

**Oak Park District 97 Releases 2025-26 Identity & Heritage Calendar**

Oak Park Elementary School District 97 has announced its Identity & Heritage Calendar for the 2025-26 school year, designed to recognize and celebrate diverse student communities throughout the academic year. The calendar was developed collaboratively with community input as part of the district's commitment to inclusion and awareness, honoring various heritage months and identity acknowledgments.

The calendar highlights key observances including Black History Month (February), Hispanic/LatinX Heritage Month (September 15-October 15), Asian American and Pacific Islander Heritage Month (May), and Native American Heritage Month (November). It also recognizes LGBTQ+ Pride Month (June) and History Month (October), Disability Pride Month (July), and various religious and cultural observances. Notable dates include Martin Luther King Jr. Day (January 20), Indigenous Peoples' Day (October 13), and International Women's Day (March 8), with some serving as official school holidays.

District 97 acknowledges that while the calendar may not be exhaustive, it welcomes additions and conducts annual reviews to ensure continued representation of all student groups. The district also emphasizes the importance of religious observances and encourages families to coordinate with schools regarding student needs related to fasting, prayer, or absences during major holidays.
