# Weekly Wrap-Up: Oct. 24, 2025

**Source:** https://mailchi.mp/op97/district-97-weekly-wrapup-4890073
**Date:** October 24, 2025

## Summary

The Oak Park community is offering Early Childhood (EC) Screening services for children ages 3 to 5, providing comprehensive developmental assessments in key areas including hearing, vision, coordination, speech, cognitive development, and social-emotional skills. Parents can schedule appointments by calling 708.524.3134.

This screening program serves as a valuable resource for families, offering parents the opportunity to consult with early childhood professionals about their child's development and ask questions about any concerns. The service is designed to identify children who may need additional support before starting school, helping to address potential developmental factors that could interfere with learning and growth. For Oak Park residents with young children, this represents an important tool for ensuring their children are developmentally ready for the school experience.
