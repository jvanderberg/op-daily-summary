# Weekly Wrap-Up: Dec. 12, 2025

**Source:** https://mailchi.mp/op97/district-97-weekly-wrapup-4890175
**Date:** December 12, 2025

## Summary

District 97 is hosting a Family Information Session on Early Entrance to help parents understand the process for enrolling children early in kindergarten or first grade. The bilingual event will take place on January 28, 2026, at 6 p.m. at Julian Middle School, located at 416 S Ridgeland Ave.

The session will feature a presentation outlining the specific process and requirements for early entrance into kindergarten or first grade. For families who cannot attend the in-person event, the district has made the presentation and all necessary information available on their website, ensuring all interested parents have access to this important educational information.
