# Weekly Wrap-Up: Jan. 23, 2026

**Source:** https://mailchi.mp/op97/district-97-weekly-wrapup-4890275
**Date:** January 23, 2026

## Summary

District 97's PTAC Committee is hosting a Community Conversation focused on understanding and supporting student behavior on February 25, 2026, from 6:30-8 p.m. at Brooks Middle School. The event requires RSVPs by February 13, 2026, and materials are provided in both English and Spanish to ensure broad community accessibility.

The conversation aims to strengthen partnerships between families, staff, and the wider Oak Park community around student behavior support while fostering meaningful dialogue about behavioral expectations, support systems, and discipline practices across the district. The event will provide a safe, inclusive space for participants to share experiences and concerns while equipping families and staff with practical tools to better support students socially, emotionally, and behaviorally.

This initiative reflects District 97's commitment to transparency, partnership, and shared responsibility in addressing student behavior challenges. By bringing together parents, guardians, board members, students, and district staff, the conversation emphasizes collaborative problem-solving and proactive strategies, giving Oak Park families and educators a direct voice in shaping how the district supports student success.
