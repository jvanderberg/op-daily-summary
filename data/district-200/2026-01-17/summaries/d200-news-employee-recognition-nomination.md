## Summary

**Oak Park School District Seeks Nominations for Employee Recognition Program**

Oak Park and River Forest High School (OPRF) has announced an employee recognition program seeking nominations for outstanding educators and staff members. The district is asking community members to nominate teachers, staff members, administrators, guest teachers, or volunteers who have demonstrated exceptional dedication to students.

The recognition program aims to honor individuals who have "gone above and beyond" in their service to OPRF students. Community members can submit nominations for any school personnel who they believe has made a significant positive impact on the school environment and student experience. This initiative reflects the district's commitment to acknowledging the valuable contributions of its educational team and volunteers who support student success at Oak Park and River Forest High School.
