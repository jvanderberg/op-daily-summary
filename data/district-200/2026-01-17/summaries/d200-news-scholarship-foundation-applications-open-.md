## Summary

The Oak Park Scholarship Foundation has opened its application process for graduating students, offering a diverse array of scholarship opportunities. The foundation recognizes various criteria including academic achievements, character, personal goals, and financial needs when awarding scholarships.

The scholarships cover multiple fields of study, including engineering, healthcare, trades, the arts, STEM fields, performing arts, and athletics. This broad range ensures that students with different interests and career paths can find relevant financial support for their educational pursuits.

For Oak Park residents, this announcement provides valuable opportunities for graduating students to receive financial assistance for their post-secondary education. Families can access the full list of available scholarships and submit applications through the foundation's application portal.
