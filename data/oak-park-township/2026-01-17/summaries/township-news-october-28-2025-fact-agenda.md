# October 28, 2025 FACT Agenda

**Source:** https://oakparktownship.org/2025/10/october-28-2025-fact-agenda/
**Date:** October 24, 2025

## Summary

The Oak Park Township Financial Advisory Committee has scheduled a meeting for Tuesday, October 28, 2025, at 6:00 pm at the Township office located at 105 S. Oak Park Ave. The meeting will be available both in-person and remotely via Zoom, with access details provided for residents who wish to participate online or by phone.

The primary focus of this meeting will be a presentation of the proposed 2025 Property Tax Levy, which is a significant agenda item that could impact Oak Park residents' property tax obligations for the coming year. The meeting will also include standard procedural items such as public comments and brief reports from the Township Manager and Supervisor.

This meeting provides an important opportunity for Oak Park residents to learn about and potentially comment on the township's proposed tax levy before it is finalized. The next Financial Advisory Committee meeting is scheduled for November 25, 2025, continuing the township's regular monthly meeting schedule.
