# October 28, 2025 Board Agenda & Packet

**Source:** https://oakparktownship.org/2025/10/october-28-2025-board-agenda-packet/
**Date:** October 25, 2025

## Summary

Oak Park Township has scheduled a Board of Trustees meeting for Tuesday, October 28, 2025, at 7:00 pm at the Township office located at 105 S. Oak Park Ave. The meeting will be available both in-person and remotely via Zoom, providing residents multiple ways to participate in local governance.

The agenda includes several significant items that will directly impact Oak Park residents. Key business items include the approval of revisions to the Personnel Policy and a presentation of the 2025 Property Tax Levy, which will affect residents' property tax obligations. The board will also consider a notable resolution prohibiting the use of Township property for civil immigration enforcement activities, and will vote on appointing Stephanie Kiesling to the Youth and Family Services Commission.

The meeting will provide opportunities for public comment and include reports from various township committees that oversee community services such as senior services, youth and family programs, and mental health initiatives. An executive session will follow the general meeting to discuss personnel matters and potential litigation, with the next regular meeting scheduled for November 25, 2025.
