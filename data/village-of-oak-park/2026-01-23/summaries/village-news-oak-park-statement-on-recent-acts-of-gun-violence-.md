# Oak Park Statement on Recent Acts of Gun Violence and Hatred

**Source:** https://www.oak-park.us/News-articles/Village-of-Oak-Park-Statement-on-Recent-Acts-of-Gun-Violence-and-Hatred
**Date:** N/A

## Summary

The Village of Oak Park issued an official statement on December 17, 2025, expressing solidarity with communities affected by two recent acts of gun violence: a tragic shooting at Brown University and an attack at a Chanukah celebration at Bondi Beach, Australia, that specifically targeted the Jewish community. The village condemned these acts as motivated by antisemitism and hatred, calling them "unconscionable" and emphasizing that such violence has no place in the global community.

In their statement, Oak Park officials unequivocally condemned all forms of antisemitism, racism, and hatred while standing united with Jewish communities in the United States, Australia, and worldwide. The village reaffirmed its commitment to fostering safety, compassion, and respect for all residents and called for collective action to build a more peaceful and just world.

For Oak Park residents, this statement reflects the community's progressive values and commitment to standing against hatred and violence. The village's response demonstrates its dedication to maintaining Oak Park as a safe, inclusive community while showing solidarity with affected communities globally during these tragic events.
