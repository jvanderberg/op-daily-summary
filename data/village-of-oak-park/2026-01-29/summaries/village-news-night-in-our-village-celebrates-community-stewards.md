# Night in Our Village Celebrates Community Stewardship

**Source:** https://www.oak-park.us/News-articles/Night-in-Our-Village-Celebrates-Community-Stewardship
**Date:** N/A

## Summary

The Oak Park community celebrated outstanding property improvements at the 2025 Cavalcade of Pride Awards, part of "A Night in Our Village" ceremony. The awards recognized excellence across multiple categories including Single Family Residential, Multi-Family Residential, and Commercial properties.

Six residential properties received recognition for their exceptional improvements: 1019 N. Kenilworth Avenue, 1201 Forest Avenue, 622 N. Marion Street, 152 Le Moyne Parkway, 227 N. Lombard Avenue, and 520 S. Harvey Avenue. These awards highlight the community's commitment to maintaining and enhancing Oak Park's architectural character and neighborhood aesthetics.

The Cavalcade of Pride Awards serve to acknowledge residents and property owners who have made significant investments in improving their properties, contributing to the overall beauty and value of Oak Park neighborhoods. The ceremony celebrates the collaborative efforts of community members in preserving and enhancing the village's distinctive character.
