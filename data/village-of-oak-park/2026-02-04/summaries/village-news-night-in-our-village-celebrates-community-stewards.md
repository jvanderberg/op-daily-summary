# Night in Our Village celebrates community stewardship

**Source:** https://www.oak-park.us/News-articles/Night-in-Our-Village-celebrates-community-stewardship
**Date:** N/A

## Summary

The Village of Oak Park held its annual "A Night in Our Village" 2025 Awards ceremony, featuring the Cavalcade of Pride Awards that recognize outstanding properties in the community. The awards program honored properties in three main categories: Single Family Residential, Multi-Family Residential, and Commercial developments.

Six residential properties were recognized with Cavalcade of Pride Awards for 2025, including homes located at 1019 N. Kenilworth Avenue, 1201 Forest Avenue, 622 N. Marion Street, 152 Le Moyne Parkway, 227 N. Lombard Avenue, and 520 S. Harvey Avenue. These awards celebrate property owners who have made significant contributions to maintaining and enhancing Oak Park's architectural character and neighborhood appeal.

The awards ceremony represents Oak Park's commitment to recognizing residents and businesses that invest in improving the community's visual appeal and property standards. For Oak Park residents, these awards highlight exemplary properties that contribute to the village's reputation for well-maintained neighborhoods and architectural preservation.
