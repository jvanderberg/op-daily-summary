# Oak Park Statement on Recent Acts of Gun Violence and Hatred

**Source:** https://www.oak-park.us/News-articles/Village-of-Oak-Park-Statement-on-Recent-Acts-of-Gun-Violence-and-Hatred
**Date:** N/A

## Summary

The Village of Oak Park released an official statement on December 17, 2025, expressing profound sorrow and solidarity following two recent tragic incidents involving gun violence and hatred. The statement addressed a shooting at Brown University and an attack at Bondi Beach, Australia, where gunfire targeted a Jewish community's Chanukah celebration. Oak Park officials condemned these acts as unconscionable attacks motivated by antisemitism and hatred.

In their statement, village leaders emphasized their commitment to confronting ideologies that fuel hatred and division, while unequivocally condemning all forms of antisemitism, racism, and hatred. The village expressed solidarity with Jewish communities in the United States, Australia, and worldwide, reaffirming their dedication to fostering safety, compassion, and respect for all residents. For Oak Park residents, this statement reinforces the community's progressive values and commitment to creating an inclusive, peaceful environment that stands against violence and bigotry in all its forms.
