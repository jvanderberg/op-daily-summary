# Oak Park achieves CDP ‘A’ score for environmental leadership

**Source:** https://www.oak-park.us/News-articles/Oak-Park-achieves-CDP-‘A’-score-for-environmental-leadership
**Date:** N/A

## Summary

Oak Park has earned a prestigious 'A' score from CDP, a global environmental non-profit organization, placing it on the annual 'A' List for environmental leadership. CDP operates the world's only independent environmental disclosure system, and Oak Park is notably the only municipality in Illinois to receive this distinction in 2025. The achievement reflects the village's comprehensive sustainability efforts under its "Climate Ready Oak Park" plan.

To earn the 'A' rating, Oak Park met rigorous leadership criteria including publicly disclosing environmental data, maintaining a city-wide emissions inventory, publishing a credible climate action plan, and completing a full climate risk assessment. The village also demonstrated additional leadership through securing formal political commitment to advance climate initiatives and deliver low-carbon development.

This recognition highlights Oak Park's commitment to environmental transparency and climate action, positioning the community among global leaders in sustainability. According to Chief Sustainability Officer Lindsey Roland Nieratka, the achievement energizes the village to continue advancing bold climate initiatives that protect the environment and enhance resident well-being. The designation underscores Oak Park's role in demonstrating how local governments can effectively address climate challenges through data-driven action and comprehensive planning.
