Village of Oak Park

Oak Park celebrated its top property improvements at the 2025 Cavalcade of Pride Awards, recognizing six residential properties and several commercial properties for exceptional upgrades that enhance the village's architectural character. The Village is taking action on several fronts to keep residents safe and informed. With dangerous cold weather hitting the area, the Public Library branches are serving as warming centers, and officials urge residents to check on neighbors while following safety guidelines like layering clothing and protecting pipes from freezing.

Oak Park achieved significant recognition by earning an 'A' score from CDP, making it the only Illinois municipality on the organization's 2025 environmental leadership list. The Village also approved its $223 million budget for 2026, which includes modest increases in utility rates - refuse collection will rise to about $31 monthly for 96-gallon carts, while water and sewer rates increase slightly. Residents need to register for the new emergency alert system at oak-park.us/notifyme, as the Village has switched to a new Everbridge platform that allows customized notification preferences.
